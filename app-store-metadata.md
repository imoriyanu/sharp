## Description (4000 chars max)

Most professionals know what to say. Few know how to say it well under pressure.

Sharp is an AI-powered communication coach that trains you to speak with clarity, structure, and substance — in 30 seconds or less per day.

Whether you are preparing for a high-stakes interview, a board presentation, or a difficult conversation with your team, Sharp gives you realistic practice and instant, personalized feedback that actually makes you better.

HOW IT WORKS

Sharp asks you a question based on real industry news, your role, and your career context. You answer out loud. Our AI scores your response across five dimensions and delivers a single, actionable coaching insight you can apply immediately.

No theory. No lectures. Just reps.

FOUR WAYS TO TRAIN

Daily 30
One question. Thirty seconds. Every day. The fastest way to build a speaking habit. Everyone gets the same question, so you can compare with friends.

One Shot
A full 90-second response with detailed scoring and coaching. Choose your difficulty. Get deep feedback on structure, concision, substance, filler words, and contextual awareness.

Threaded Challenge
Four rounds of escalating follow-up questions that push your thinking. Start with a prompt, then face three increasingly difficult follow-ups — just like a real interview or Q&A session. Ends with a comprehensive debrief.

Sharp Duels
Challenge a friend to answer the same question. Record your response, share the link, and compare scores side by side. The best way to stay accountable and make practice competitive.

AI SCORING ON 5 DIMENSIONS

Every response is scored across five dimensions by our AI coaching engine:

Structure — Did you lead with the answer and organize your thoughts logically?
Concision — Did you make your point without rambling or repeating yourself?
Substance — Did you go beyond surface-level and provide real evidence or insight?
Filler Words — Did you speak cleanly, without ums, uhs, and crutch phrases?
Awareness — Did you demonstrate knowledge of your industry, company, or audience?

Your scores are tracked over time so you can see exactly where you are improving and where you need more reps.

PERSONALIZED TO YOUR CAREER

Upload your CV, job descriptions, project briefs, or meeting agendas. Sharp uses your documents to generate questions that mirror the real conversations you face at work. Practice answering the questions that actually matter to your career.

COACHING THAT STICKS

After every response, Sharp delivers one memorable coaching insight — the single most impactful thing you can do differently next time. Our coaching engine is grounded in proven communication science frameworks, distilled into advice that is practical and immediately actionable.

No jargon. No book recommendations. Just the insight.
