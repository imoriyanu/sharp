Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 28, 2026

Sharp AI ("Sharp," "we," "our," or "us") provides a communication training application (the "App") designed to help professionals improve how they speak under pressure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using Sharp, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide voluntarily. Sharp may ask you to enter contextual information such as your role, company, or industry to personalize coaching feedback. You are never required to provide this information, and it is stored locally on your device only.

Audio recordings. When you use a practice session, the App records your spoken responses. These recordings are transmitted to our servers solely for transcription and AI-based scoring. Audio data is processed in real time and is not stored on our servers after processing is complete.

Session data. Scores, coaching feedback, transcripts, and session history are generated during your use of the App and stored locally on your device using on-device storage (AsyncStorage). This data is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Sharp does not require you to create an account. There is no username, password, email address, or other personal identifier collected during normal use of the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • Transcription: Converting your spoken audio into text so it can be analyzed.
  • AI scoring and coaching: Evaluating your responses across dimensions such as structure, concision, substance, filler word usage, and contextual awareness, and generating actionable coaching feedback.
  • Text-to-speech: Generating spoken audio for practice questions and prompts within the App.
  • Personalization: Using optional context you provide (role, company, documents) to make coaching feedback more relevant.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to delivering the core functionality of the App.

3. Third-Party Services

Sharp relies on the following third-party services to deliver its features. Each service receives only the minimum data necessary to perform its function:

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — Receives transcribed text and optional context to generate scores and coaching feedback. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.
  • Groq (Whisper Large v3 Turbo) — Receives audio data for speech-to-text transcription. Groq's privacy policy is available at groq.com/privacy-policy.
  • ElevenLabs — Generates text-to-speech audio for practice questions. ElevenLabs receives text prompts only and does not receive any user audio or personal data. Their privacy policy is available at elevenlabs.io/privacy-policy.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

All session data, scores, transcripts, and user-provided context are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device except when audio is temporarily transmitted for transcription and scoring as described above.

Audio recordings are transmitted securely over HTTPS to our processing servers and to the third-party services listed above. Audio is processed in memory and is not written to persistent storage on our servers.

If you delete the App from your device, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

5. Data Retention

Because Sharp stores data locally on your device, you have full control over your data at all times. We do not maintain copies of your session history, transcripts, or scores on any server.

Audio recordings are retained only for the duration of the transcription and scoring process, typically a matter of seconds, after which they are discarded.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access and deletion: All your data is stored on your device. You can view it within the App or delete it by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.
  • Opt out of audio recording: You may choose not to use practice features that require audio input. The App will not record without your active initiation.
  • Withdraw optional context: You may remove any role, company, or document information you have provided at any time through the App's settings.

If you have questions about your data or wish to make a request, please contact us at privacy@speaksharpai.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Sharp is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@speaksharpai.com and we will take steps to address the situation.

8. International Data Transfers

Audio data transmitted for transcription and scoring may be processed by third-party services located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of this data to servers that may be located in the United States or other jurisdictions. All transfers are conducted over encrypted connections.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page and may notify you through the App. Your continued use of Sharp after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
