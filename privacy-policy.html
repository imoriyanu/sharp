Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026

Sharp AI ("Sharp," "we," "our," or "us") provides a communication training application (the "App") designed to help professionals improve how they speak under pressure. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using Sharp, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you sign in with Apple Sign In, we receive your Apple user identifier and, if you choose to share it, your name and email address. Apple may provide a private relay email address instead of your real email. We use this information solely to create and manage your account.

b) Profile and Context Information

You may voluntarily provide information such as your name, job role, company, career situation, and professional goals to personalise coaching feedback. You may also upload professional documents (CVs, job descriptions, project briefs) for more tailored practice scenarios. This information is stored on your device and synced to your account if you are signed in.

c) Audio Recordings

When you use a practice session, the App records your spoken responses. These recordings are transmitted to our servers solely for transcription and AI-based scoring. Audio data is processed in real time and is not permanently stored on our servers after processing is complete.

In Conversation mode, live audio is streamed directly to ElevenLabs for real-time conversational AI interaction. This audio is processed in real time and is not stored after the session ends.

d) Session Data

Scores, coaching feedback, transcripts, streaks, badges, and session history are generated during your use of the App. This data is stored locally on your device and synced to your account via our cloud service (Supabase) if you are signed in.

e) Purchase Information

If you subscribe to Sharp Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and verify entitlements. We do not have access to your payment card details or bank account information. RevenueCat receives your anonymous app user ID and purchase receipt to validate your subscription.

f) Usage and Analytics Data

We collect anonymised usage data to understand how the App is used and to improve the product. This includes events such as app launches, feature usage, session completions, and paywall interactions. This data is collected via PostHog and is not linked to your identity for advertising purposes.

g) Push Notification Tokens

If you opt in to push notifications, we collect your device push token to send you reminders and engagement notifications. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

h) Diagnostics

We may collect crash logs and performance data through Expo to diagnose technical issues and improve app stability.

Sharp does not collect precise location, contacts, browsing history, health data, financial information, or any sensitive personal information as defined by applicable privacy laws.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: Verifying your identity when you sign in with Apple.
  • Transcription: Converting your spoken audio into text for analysis.
  • AI Scoring and Coaching: Evaluating your responses across dimensions such as structure, concision, substance, filler word usage, and contextual awareness, and generating actionable coaching feedback.
  • Conversational AI: Powering real-time voice conversations with AI agents for practice scenarios.
  • Text-to-Speech: Generating spoken audio for practice questions and model answers.
  • Personalisation: Using optional context you provide (role, company, documents) to make coaching feedback and practice scenarios more relevant.
  • Subscription Management: Verifying your Sharp Pro subscription status and unlocking premium features.
  • Analytics: Understanding how the App is used to improve features and user experience.
  • Notifications: Sending practice reminders and engagement prompts if you have opted in.
  • Diagnostics: Identifying and resolving technical issues.

We do not use your information for third-party advertising, profiling for advertising purposes, or any purpose unrelated to delivering and improving the App.

5. Data Storage and Security

Session data, scores, transcripts, and user-provided context are stored locally on your device using on-device storage (AsyncStorage). If you are signed in, this data is also synced to Supabase, a secure cloud database hosted on AWS infrastructure.

Audio recordings are transmitted securely over HTTPS (TLS encryption) to our processing servers and to the third-party services listed above. Audio is processed in memory and is not written to persistent storage on our servers after processing.

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit, secure server infrastructure, and access controls on our backend systems.

6. Data Retention

  • Audio recordings: Retained only for the duration of transcription and scoring (typically seconds), then discarded.
  • Session data: Stored on your device indefinitely until you delete it. Cloud-synced data is retained while your account is active.
  • Account data: Retained while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.
  • Analytics data: Retained in anonymised form for up to 24 months.
  • Push notification tokens: Retained while notifications are enabled. Removed when you disable notifications or delete your account.

If you delete the App from your device, all locally stored data is permanently removed. To delete cloud-synced data, please contact us.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal data. Locally stored data can be deleted by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.
  • Correction: Update your profile information at any time within the App.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt out of audio recording: You may choose not to use practice features that require audio input. The App will not record without your active initiation.
  • Opt out of notifications: Disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.
  • Opt out of analytics: Contact us to opt out of analytics data collection.
  • Withdraw context: Remove any role, company, or document information you have provided at any time through the App's settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@sharp-ai.app. We will respond to your request within 30 days.
