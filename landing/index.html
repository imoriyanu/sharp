AI Scoring

Five dimensions. Zero guesswork.

Every answer is scored on the dimensions that actually matter. Not "good job" — real numbers, real criticism, real coaching grounded in communication science.

Structure
— Did you lead with the answer, not the backstory?
Concision
— Could you have said it in half the words?
Substance
— Did you use evidence, numbers, specifics?
Filler Words
— How many "ums," "likes," and "basicallys"?
Awareness
— Do you understand your industry context?
